Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services, booking process, and policies.

Booking an appointment at Barberian is easy! You can book online through our website by clicking the "Book Now" button, call us directly at (123) 456-7890, or use our mobile app. During the booking process, you'll be able to select your preferred barber, service, date, and time.

We understand that plans can change. We ask that you give us at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. This allows us to offer the time slot to another client. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice may be subject to a cancellation fee of 50% of the service price.

A standard haircut typically takes about 30-45 minutes. However, the duration can vary depending on the complexity of the style, hair length, and additional services requested. Beard trims usually take 15-20 minutes, while a full service including haircut, beard trim, and styling can take up to an hour.

Yes, we do accept walk-ins based on availability. However, we highly recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure you get your preferred barber, date, and time. Walk-in clients are served on a first-come, first-served basis, and wait times can vary depending on how busy we are.

We accept various payment methods including cash, all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and mobile payment options like Apple Pay and Google Pay. We also offer gift cards that can be purchased online or in-store.

Still have questions? We're here to help!
