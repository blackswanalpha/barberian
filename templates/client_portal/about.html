Our Story

{% if business_settings and business_settings.about %} {{ business_settings.about|linebreaks }} {% else %}

Founded in 2010, Barberian began with a simple vision: to create a space where men could enjoy premium grooming services in a relaxed, welcoming environment.

What started as a small shop with just two chairs has grown into a renowned establishment, known for its exceptional service, skilled barbers, and commitment to the craft of barbering.

Over the years, we've built a loyal community of clients who trust us with their style. We've expanded our services, refined our techniques, and created a brand that stands for quality and excellence.

Today, Barberian continues to honor the traditions of classic barbering while embracing modern styles and techniques. We're proud of our journey and excited about the future.
