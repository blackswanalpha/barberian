Our Story

Founded in 2024, Barberian was born out of a passion for exceptional grooming and a desire to create a space where men could not only get a great haircut but also enjoy a premium experience.

What started as a small shop with just two chairs has grown into one of Nairobi's most respected barbershops, known for our attention to detail, skilled barbers, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Over the years, we've built a loyal clientele who appreciate our blend of traditional barbering techniques and modern styles. We're proud to be a part of the community and look forward to continuing to serve our clients for many years to come.

Our Mission

At Barberian, our mission is to provide exceptional grooming services in a comfortable and welcoming environment. We strive to exceed our clients' expectations with every visit, ensuring they leave looking and feeling their best.

Excellence
